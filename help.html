😊 Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I verify my account?

Join our Discord server and go to the #🟠・verify channel. Follow the instructions to complete your verification. Verified users get access to giveaways and special events.

2. Where do I submit my Rainbet account?

After verification, go to #📄・account-submission-guide and post your Rainbet username registered under tzbetz.

3. What are OG and VIP badges?

These badges are awarded on Kick livestreams, not Rainbet. OG = 2+ months following. VIP = 3 gifted subs or 2+ months of subscribing. Both unlock giveaways and Wheel Spins.

4. What happens if I don’t claim a giveaway win?

You have 24 hours to claim unless stated otherwise. Failure to verify or respond in time may lead to disqualification and a re-roll.

5. Can I use multiple accounts?

No. Multiple entries from the same person, household, or IP address are not allowed. Alt accounts are strictly disqualified.

6. How do I get support?

Please create a ticket in the Discord server using the #📅シsupport or ticket channel. Do not send emails. The staff team will respond as soon as possible.
